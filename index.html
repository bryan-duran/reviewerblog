Mexico

Mexico has an extensive history with wonderful architecture and an incredible sense of culture. Dating back to BCE 2500-400, one of the first empires within Mesoameria were the Olmecs.

Food

Mexico's gastronomy comes from a blend of indigenous, European, and African roots. Before colonization, the indigenous population used the "three sisters" of vegetables; corn, squash, and beans.
